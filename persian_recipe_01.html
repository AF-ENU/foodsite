Koobideh Kebab

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, grated
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef or lamb, grated onion, bread crumbs, egg, salt, black pepper, cumin, turmeric, paprika, and garlic powder. Mix well to combine.
  2. Knead the mixture for 10-15 minutes until it is well combined and the meat becomes sticky.
  3. Divide the mixture into 6-8 equal portions.
  4. Take one portion and form it around a skewer, flattening the meat around the skewer to create a long, thin sausage shape.
  5. Repeat with the remaining portions of meat.
  6. Grill the kebabs over medium-high heat for 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are browned and cooked through.
  7. Serve hot with lavash bread, grilled tomatoes, and fresh herbs.
