Lahm Bi Ajin

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground lamb or beef
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup parsley, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp. tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp. pomegranate molasses
  • 1 tbsp. lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp. sumac
  • 1 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. black pepper
  • 1 tsp. cumin
  • 1 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp. allspice
  • 1/2 tsp. nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
  • 1 lb. pizza dough
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts, toasted

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 F.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together ground lamb or beef, onion, parsley, tomato paste, pomegranate molasses, lemon juice, sumac, olive oil, salt, black pepper, cumin, cinnamon, allspice, nutmeg, and cayenne pepper until well combined.
  3. Divide pizza dough into 6 equal pieces and roll out each piece into a thin circle. Place each circle on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Spread the meat mixture over each circle, leaving a 1/2 inch border around the edges. Sprinkle with toasted pine nuts.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until crust is golden brown and meat is cooked through.
