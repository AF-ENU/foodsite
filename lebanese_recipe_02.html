Muhammara

Ingredients

  • 2 red bell peppers, seeded and chopped
  • 1 cup walnuts, chopped
  • 1 cup fresh bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon pomegranate molasses
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Place the chopped bell peppers, walnuts, bread crumbs, olive oil, pomegranate molasses, lemon juice, garlic, cumin, smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, and salt in a food processor. Process until smooth.
  3. Transfer the muhammara mixture to an oven-safe dish and bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until warmed through.
  4. Serve warm or at room temperature with pita bread, crackers, or vegetables.
